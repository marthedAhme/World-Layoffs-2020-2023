# World Layoffs 2020–2023

**Overview**
This project analyzes global all-sector layoff events documented between 2020 and 2023. Starting from a raw CSV dataset of 2,361 records across 9 columns, the project follows a full end-to-end data analysis pipeline — from raw data ingestion to a clean, analyzed, and visualized output ready for decision-makers.
The dataset covers layoff events from 51 countries across 31 industries, documenting the scale of workforce reductions during one of the most turbulent periods in tech history — spanning the COVID-19 pandemic, the post-pandemic hiring boom, and the subsequent market correction driven by rising interest rates.
